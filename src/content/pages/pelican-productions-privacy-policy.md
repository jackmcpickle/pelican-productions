Pelican Productions respects your right to privacy. We are committed to managing
your information in a respectful way.

## Collection

- We collect personal information only if you have consented or if it is
  reasonably necessary for our functions and activities, such as providing
  services or addressing accounts to you. This information may include your
  name, addresses, phone numbers and billing information.
- We will collect information directly from you or your authorised
  representative unless it is unreasonable or impracticable to do so.
- We collect information by lawful and fair means only.

## Use and disclosure of your personal information

- We use or disclose your information for the purpose you have provided it for
  or for directly related purposes you would reasonably expect. This may include
  sharing your information with casting agents or other arts-related businesses
  with your prior permission.
- You may request not to receive marketing or promotional information from
  Pelican Productions at any time by contacting us.
- We may be required to disclose your information in response to legal processes
  or requests by law enforcement agencies.

## Quality and security of your personal information

- We take reasonable steps to ensure the information we hold is accurate,
  complete, and up to date.
- We store information securely and protect it from misuse, interference, loss,
  and unauthorised access or disclosure.

## Access and correction

- You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
- If the information is inaccurate, out-of-date, or incomplete, you may request
  that we correct it.

## Complaints

If you have a complaint about how we handle your information, please contact us
and we will respond. You may also contact the Office of the Australian
Information Commissioner:
http://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/making-a-privacy-complaint.
