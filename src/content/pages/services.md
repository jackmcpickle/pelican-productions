Pelican Productions offers a broad range of services for young performers, adult
artists, and community groups.

### Training and Classes

- Pelican Academy and Vocal Academy programs for young performers.
- SMASH music theatre dance classes for ages 7–17.
- Acting classes focused on performance skills and confidence.
- Sing!Oz community choir for adults.
- Pop Academy classes for ages 7–12.

### Intensive Programs

- Music Theatre Camp (MTC) and Spotlight holiday intensives.
- Certificate IV in Performing Arts for students seeking professional training.

### Production and Hire

- Musical theatre productions and performances throughout the year.
- Set, prop, lighting, and costume hire for schools and theatre groups.

For current schedules and pricing, please contact Pelican Productions.
