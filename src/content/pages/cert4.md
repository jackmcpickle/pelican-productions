The Certificate IV in Performing Arts is a one-year, nationally accredited
course for students who are serious about pursuing professional performing arts
training.

Delivered by a team of industry professionals, the course focuses on acting,
voice, and dance, with an emphasis on ensemble performance, audition
preparation, and professional readiness.

Upon completion, students are eligible to audition for specialist tertiary
programs or enter the performing arts workforce.

For enrolment information and current fees, please contact Pelican Productions.
