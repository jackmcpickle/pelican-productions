Pop Academy is a dance, singing, and performance class designed for young
performers aged 7–12. Classes focus on popular music styles and performance
skills while building confidence in a fun, supportive environment.

Led by Rachel Mary Wilson, Pop Academy helps students develop stagecraft,
musicality, and performance confidence, including camera awareness for pop-style
performance.

For current class times and fees, please contact Pelican Productions.
