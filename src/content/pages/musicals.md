Pelican Productions has staged a wide range of musical theatre productions.
Highlights include:

- 2007: _The Wizard of Oz_
- 2008: _The Sound of Music_
- 2009: _Into the Woods_
- 2010: _Grease_
- 2011: _Oliver_
- 2012: _Hairspray_
- 2013: _Grease_
- 2014: _Disney’s The Little Mermaid JR_
- 2015: _Chitty Chitty Bang Bang_
- 2016: _Cats_
- 2017: _Bring It On – The Musical_
- 2017: _Seussical the Musical_
- 2018: _Annie JR_
- 2018: _The Pirates of Penzance_
- 2019: _In the Heights_
- 2019: _Disney’s The Little Mermaid JR_
